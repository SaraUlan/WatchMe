Categories

Category Quantity Definition
Horror 17 Horror is a film genre that seeks to elicit
fear or disgust in its audience for entertainment purposes.
Horror films often explore dark subject matter and may deal with
transgressive topics or themes. brBroad elements include monsters,
apocalyptic events, and religious or folk beliefs
Romance 25 Romance films involve romantic love stories recorded in visual media
for broadcast in theatres or on television that focus on passion, emotion,
and the affectionate romantic involvement of the main characters. Typically
their journey through dating, courtship or marriage is featured.
Action 15 Action film is a film genre in which the protagonist is thrust into a series of
events that typically involve violence and physical feats
Drama 23 In film and television, drama is a category or genre of narrative fiction intended
to be more serious than humorous in tone.
Thriller 29 Thriller film, also known as suspense film or suspense thriller, is a broad film genre
that evokes excitement and suspense in the audience. The suspense element found
in most films' plots is particularly exploited by the filmmaker in this genre.
Comedy 64 A comedy film is a category of film which emphasizes on humor. These films are designed
to make the audience laugh in amusement. Films in this style traditionally have a happy ending.
Comedy is one of the oldest genres in film and it is derived from classical comedy in theatre
Sports 32 A sports film is a film genre in which any particular sport plays a prominent role in the film's plot
or acts as its central theme. It is a production in which a sport or a sports-related topic is prominently
featured or is a focus of the plot.
Mystery 33 A mystery film is a genre of film that revolves around the solution of a problem or a crime. It focuses on the
efforts of the detective, private investigator or amateur sleuth to solve the mysterious circumstances of an
issue by means of clues, investigation, and clever deduction.
Historical 21 A fiction film showing past events or set within a historical period.
